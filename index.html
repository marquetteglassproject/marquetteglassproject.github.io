Find Out More


Marquette Glass Project is aiming to reduce glass waste in the Marquette area, while simultaneously providing a creative outlet for individuals to express themselves.

PROCESS

How It Works

Collection

Glass is collected.

Processing

Broken down and sorted.

Repurposing

Shards are melted and reused.

Creation

Recycled glass is used for art.

Our Vision

Establish a new program in the Art and Design department at Northern Michigan University focused on giving students a creative outlet to turn recycled glass into works of art. This vision first came to us as we were challenged by our professor, Teresa Bertossi, to make a sustainable change in our community. So, we decided to feed off our frustration with the city for not recycling glass and turn our glass blowing idea into a class project. But for us, it has become so much more than a grade; we intend to see our vision through because not only are we passionate, but we are discontent.
